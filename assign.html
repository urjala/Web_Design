SELENA GOMEZ

About her

Date: December 16 th 2022

Actress and singer Selena Gomez was born on July 22, 1992 in Grand Prairie, Texas.
She is the daughter of Mandy Teefey and Ricardo Gomez.Her mother is of
part Italian ancestry, and her father is of Mexican descent. She was named after
Tejano singer Selena, who died in 1995.
