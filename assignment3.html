Marvel

Marvel's The Avengers or simply The Avengers, is a 2012 American superhero film based on the
Marvel Comics superhero team of the same name.

Directors: Joss Whedon, Joe Russo, Anthony Russo, Cate Shortland

Production companies: Marvel Studios, Walt Disney Pictures, Walt Disney Studios Motion Pictures
